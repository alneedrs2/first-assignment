A personal mission statement can help you identify your values and goals, defining what matters most to you professionally. It makes decision-making easier as well, as it clarifies whether the decisions you're making are in alignment with what you want for your life.

A personal vision statement can help take your life or career in the direction you want to go. Use these personal vision statement examples to help craft one of your own, tailored to your own goals and situation.
